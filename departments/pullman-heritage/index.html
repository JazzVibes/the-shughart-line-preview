Pullman Heritage

Pullman history is not just a car. It is a way of remembering travel, labor, craft, and place.

Pullman heritage carries many rooms at once: sleeping-car craft, company-town history, railroad labor, passenger service, restoration, and the public events that let people walk up to the story instead of only reading about it.

This stop keeps the romance connected to the source. It gives Pullman-curious visitors a few doors to open without making The Shughart Line sound like the owner of someone else's timetable.

Begin here, then use the cards below to reach the host source. Outside pages open in a new tab, and the host keeps the current timetable.
