Private Passenger Rail

Private varnish needs romance, but it also needs operating rules.

Private passenger rail can look like a luxury story from the outside, but the better story is stewardship. A private car carries hospitality, mechanical care, railroad rules, preservation money, and the memory of travel done with intention.

Most people will never buy the expensive ticket. This room lets them understand the craft for free, then points serious inquiries back to the official source that owns the timetable and the responsibility.
