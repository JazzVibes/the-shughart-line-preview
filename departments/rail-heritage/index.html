Rail Heritage

Rail Heritage

The real railroad story is still open to the public.

Rail heritage is the public memory of the railroad: museums, depots, preserved lines, tourist railways, tramways, archives, and local societies keeping the right-of-way from disappearing into the weeds.

This room is where The Shughart Line can do real public service. It helps a visitor find the place, understand why it matters, and then return to the host source for the timetable, ticket window, accessibility, and route details.

Begin here, then use the cards below to reach the host source. Outside pages open in a new tab, and the host keeps the current timetable.
