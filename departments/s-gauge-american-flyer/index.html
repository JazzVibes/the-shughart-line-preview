S Gauge / American Flyer

S Gauge And American Flyer

Start in the S gauge room before you take the branch line.

S gauge sits at a useful middle scale: big enough to feel mechanical and substantial, small enough to build a railroad room around a family, a club, or a lifelong collection. American Flyer gives that room a particular warmth because it carries toy-train memory and serious railroad craft at the same time.

This stop keeps the welcome inside The Shughart Line first. From here you can find club rooms, layout stories, Dispatch trails, and American Flyer source material before you open an official outside page.
