Signal And Sound

Railroads taught people to listen for the right entrance.

Signals, timetables, horns, station calls, brass bands, and model-railroad sound all belong to the same human habit: listening for order in motion. This room gives the site a place for culture without losing the railroad thread.

It also lets Larry's music life sit naturally beside his railroad life. The point is not to send a visitor abruptly to a concert page; the point is to say why sound, timing, discipline, and public service belong on the same line.
